Overview

This privacy policy applies to all applications and websites created and operated by David Gaddy.

These apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No accounts are required. No forms ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information. No analytics or tracking scripts are embedded in these applications.

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share or sell. David Gaddy does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer any user data to third parties — ever.

Some apps may use your browser's local storage solely to save your preferences (such as display settings or last-viewed content) on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Third-Party Services

These apps load certain open-source libraries from public CDNs. Those CDNs may log standard web request information (IP address, user agent) per their own policies, but David Gaddy does not control or access that data.

Children's Privacy

These apps do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no special risk to minors.
